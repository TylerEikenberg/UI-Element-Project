Founded in 1939, Marvel Comics is one of the biggest powerhouses in the comic book industry. Explore the Marvel Comic universe using this helpful tool!

X-Men

Created by artist/co-writer Jack Kirby and writer Stan Lee, the X-Men first appeared in The X-Men #1 (September 1963) and formed one of the most recognizable and successful franchises in the history of Marvel Comics.

The Avengers

The Avengers made its debut in The Avengers #1 (cover-dated Sept. 1963), created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby.

Guardians of the Galaxy

The Guardians of the Galaxy are an outlaw superhero team appearing in Marvel Comics. The Guardians first appeared in Marvel Super-Heroes #18 (Jan. 1969).
